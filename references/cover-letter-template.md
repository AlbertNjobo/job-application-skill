# Cover Letter Template

## LETTERS Dict Entry Format

Add this to `generate_cover_letters.py`:

```python
"<COMPANY_KEY>": {
    "filename": "Lawrence_Albert_Njobo_Cover_Letter_<Company>.pdf",
    "recipient": [
        "The Hiring Manager",
        "<Department or Company Name>",
        "<Company Location>",
    ],
    "re": "Re: <Role Title> (<req ID if available>)",
    "paragraphs": [
        "<Hook: problem-solver or company-knowledge opening>",
        "<Body 1: strongest qualification match with metrics>",
        "<Body 2: additional qualifications, certs, culture fit>",
        "<Closing: enthusiasm, specific contribution, call to action>",
    ],
},
```

## Rules
- Exactly 1 page
- Times New Roman: 16pt bold name centered, 12pt body
- "Dear Hiring Manager:" (colon, no Mr./Ms./Dr.)
- 3-4 paragraphs, 250-400 words
- No em dashes, no AI banned words
- Open with hook (never "I am writing to apply for...")
- Each body paragraph connects experience to JD requirement
- No zip codes in addresses
- Recipient block: each line separated

## Hook Types (pick one)
1. Problem-solver: "X problem costs companies Y. [Company]'s approach to Z is exactly..."
2. Company knowledge: "[Company]'s recent initiative on X caught my attention..."
3. Industry insight: "The [industry] space is shifting toward X, and [Company]..."
